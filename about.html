About Us

EduSeater is a next-generation college exam seating system designed to automate and optimize the seating arrangement process, ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and fairness.

Features

Automated Seating Plan

Smart seat allocation based on branch, subject, and room capacity.

Student Portal

Instantly view exam block, room, and seat details.

Admin Control Panel

Manage rooms, benches, and subject-wise seat allocation with ease.

Downloadable Reports

Export seating plans in CSV format.

Optimized Arrangements

Ensures students from the same branch or subject are not seated together.
